Our Mission

We enrich RVR & JC CoE’s impact by serving as an independent gateway for alums of all identities, backgrounds, and experiences – across the globe and in our neighborhoods – in order to create and deepen belonging to the RVR & JC CoE Family, the Leaders and Best.
